Haier Group Adjusts Refrigerator Prices in Australia, Including Fisher & Paykel Brand

Posted on 2025-04-10

Effective April 1, 2025, Haier Group has implemented a comprehensive price increase across its refrigerator product lines in the Australian market, including those under its Fisher & Paykel brand. The overall adjustment reflects an average price rise of approximately 6%~10%.

Key price changes include:
• Cross Door models (600L and above): +AUD $200
• Side by Side models: +AUD $100
• Other compact refrigerators (e.g. single-door, top/bottom mount): +AUD $50–$100


Notably, the group’s best-selling 400L Bottom Mount refrigerator retains its current pricing, signaling a strategic focus on maintaining competitiveness in core product segments.

This price revision is likely driven by rising global raw material costs and ongoing supply chain pressures. Industry observers anticipate that other major brands may follow suit with similar adjustments in the near future.

自2025年4月1日起，海尔系在澳大利亚市场全面上调冰箱产品价格，涉及其旗下Fisher & Paykel等品牌，整体涨幅约为6%~10%。

具体调整如下：600L及以上多门（Cross Door）冰箱每台上调200澳元，对开门（Side by Side）机型上调100澳元，其余小型冰箱（如单门、双门等）涨幅在50至100澳元之间不等。


值得注意的是，尽管整体价格上扬，海尔系在澳洲的主销机型——400L容量下置冷冻冰箱（Bottom Mount）仍维持原价，显示其在核心产品上的市场稳定策略。

此次价格调整可能受全球原材料成本上涨及供应链压力影响，行业预计其他主要品牌亦有可能跟进调价。

Samsung Unveils New Refrigerator Lineup: 9-inch Integrating Screens and Enhanced AI Vision Features

Posted on 2025-04-09

Release Date: March 24, 2025

On March 24, 2025, Samsung Electronics unveiled its new lineup of smart refrigerators, continuing its “Screens Everywhere” vision presented at CES 2025. The new series includes 9-inch AI home screens on four-door, French-door, and side-by-side refrigerators, and enhanced Family Hub screens on select models. Additionally, the new refrigerators feature an upgraded AI Vision Inside function, allowing better food recognition to optimize users’ kitchen experiences.

Key Product Descriptions and Features

1. 9-inch AI Home Screen


The 9-inch AI home screen on the new series enhances users’ food management, home control, communication, and entertainment experiences. With the AI Vision Inside function, the screen recognizes 37 food types, including fresh and processed items. Personalized recipe suggestions and meal planning simplify cooking. The screen also provides smart home control, allowing users to manage other connected devices through the SmartThings app. The Daily Board feature displays schedules, weather forecasts, and daily energy consumption reports, helping users organize their day-to-day activities.

2. Family Hub Large Screen

Select French-door and side-by-side models come with either a 32-inch or 21.5-inch Family Hub screen. This screen integrates the upgraded AI Vision Inside function, recognizing more food types and providing personalized recipes and meal suggestions. Users can also access the SmartThings app through the screen to control other smart devices in the home. The Daily Board function is also available on the Family Hub, providing real-time updates and information.

3. AI Vision Inside Function

The upgraded AI Vision Inside function enables the fridge to recognize up to 37 food types, including fresh and processed items. Users can store common food items for easier management and planning. This intelligent recognition feature provides personalized recipe and meal planning recommendations, improving the kitchen experience.

4. Innovative AI Hybrid Cooling Technology

The new refrigerator series features AI Hybrid Cooling Technology, combining the traditional compressor with the Peltier module to provide an intelligent cooling solution. This hybrid system ensures the fridge maintains a consistent temperature, even when there is a significant change in internal temperature, or when large quantities of groceries are added. The Peltier module assists the compressor when frequent door openings or high temperatures occur, ensuring optimal cooling performance, keeping food fresh for longer, and maximizing available space.

5. AI Energy Mode

The new refrigerators come with AI Energy Mode, allowing users to optimize energy consumption. This mode can reduce electricity use by up to 10%. Users can activate it continuously or during periods when electricity bills are expected to exceed a predefined threshold, providing better energy efficiency and cost savings.

Additional Features and Benefits

  • Smart Home Integration: The new refrigerators are deeply integrated with Samsung’s SmartThings ecosystem, allowing users to control other smart devices in their homes, such as lights and outlets, through the fridge.

  • Daily Board Feature: The Daily Board on the fridge’s screen displays schedules, weather forecasts, and daily energy consumption reports, helping users stay organized.

  • Bixby Voice Assistant: Users can control the fridge and other connected devices via voice commands using the Bixby assistant, enhancing convenience.

Conclusion

Samsung’s new smart refrigerator lineup integrates advanced screen technology, AI features, AI Hybrid Cooling, and AI Energy Mode to offer users an enhanced, intelligent kitchen experience. Whether it’s food management, home control, or entertainment, these refrigerators demonstrate Samsung’s innovation and leadership in the smart home space.

Midea Launches World's First Sideboard Refrigerator, Ushering in a New Era for Fridges

Posted on 2025-03-24 Lino Edited on 2025-04-09

On March 21, 2025, at the Appliance & Electronics World Expo (AWE2025) in China, Midea, in collaboration with JD.com, unveiled the world’s first sideboard refrigerator, marking a new era in the refrigerator industry. With its ultra-thin 45cm depth and a spacious 427L capacity, this refrigerator is the first to bring fridge thickness into the “4” era, completely revolutionizing traditional refrigerator design and filling a gap in kitchen and dining space scenarios.

AWE2025 Midea New Fridge Launch

Innovative Design Leading the Scene Revolution

Zhao Guoyao, Chief Product Expert at Midea’s Refrigerator Division, stated that as consumer lifestyles evolve, the use scenarios for refrigerators have expanded from traditional kitchens to living rooms and dining areas. However, traditional refrigerators, typically over 60cm thick, cannot seamlessly integrate with 45cm-deep sideboards, leading to inefficient space utilization and compromised aesthetics. To address this, Midea leveraged technological innovation to introduce this ultra-thin sideboard refrigerator, which not only solves spatial issues but also enhances the overall aesthetic of home interiors.

Technological Breakthroughs for Ultra-Thin, High-Capacity Design

To achieve the 45cm ultra-thin design, Midea implemented several technological innovations. First, by relocating components such as the air duct evaporator from the back of the refrigerator to the middle beam, significant space was freed up at the rear. Second, the use of self-developed environmentally friendly VIP insulation material reduced the foam layer thickness to one-third of that in conventional refrigerators, maintaining insulation performance while significantly decreasing the body thickness. Additionally, Midea pioneered a micro high-efficiency compressor, reducing its size by 30% while increasing freezing capacity by 33%, ensuring the refrigerator remains highly efficient despite its slim design.

Fine-Tuned Compartmentalization Enhances User Experience

Midea’s sideboard refrigerator not only breaks new ground in thickness and capacity but also innovates in compartmentalization. Equipped with the All-Space Flexible Storage Technology, the refrigerator features 16 scientifically divided compartments, providing users with detailed storage guidance. The innovative foldable shelves allow flexible storage for bottles of varying heights, further enhancing user convenience.

Smart Purification Ensures Food Safety

To ensure the safety of stored food, Midea’s sideboard refrigerator is equipped with PST+ Smart Purification Technology, capable of removing over 200 harmful substances, including bacteria specific to Chinese household refrigerators, broad-spectrum bacteria, viruses, ethylene, odors, pesticide residues, and mold. This seven-in-one purification system ensures that users can enjoy safer food storage.

Future Outlook: Continuous Innovation

Wang Zhe, Head of Preservation Technology at Midea’s Refrigerator Division, mentioned that Midea has filed 46 patents related to this product, ensuring a balance between ultra-thin design, high performance, and large capacity. Moving forward, Midea will continue to invest heavily in R&D, focusing on preservation, energy efficiency, and purification technologies. By leveraging its global production base and talent pool, Midea aims to strengthen its competitiveness in the refrigerator industry.

The launch of the sideboard refrigerator not only represents a differentiated new category in a mature market but also reflects Midea’s deep understanding of consumer needs and industry trends. As Chinese households increasingly demand integrated home solutions and enhanced user experiences, Midea is poised to lead the industry’s innovative development through continuous technological and product advancements.

2025年3月21日，在2025年中国家电及消费电子博览会（AWE2025）上，美的联合京东电器正式发布了全球首款餐边柜冰箱，标志着冰箱行业进入了一个全新的时代。这款冰箱以其全球最薄的45厘米厚度和427升大容量，首次将冰箱厚度带入“4”时代，彻底颠覆了传统冰箱的设计理念，填补了餐厨空间的场景空白。

AWE2025美的冰箱新品发布会

创新设计，引领场景革命

美的冰箱事业部首席产品专家赵国尧表示，随着消费者生活方式的改变，冰箱的使用场景已从传统的厨房扩展到客厅和餐厅。然而，传统冰箱的厚度通常超过60厘米，无法与45厘米深度的餐边柜完美融合，导致空间利用不充分且影响美观。为此，美的通过技术创新，推出了这款超薄餐边柜冰箱，不仅解决了空间问题，还提升了家居的整体美感。

技术突破，实现超薄高容

The Peltier Effect: A Thermoelectric Phenomenon

Discovered in 1834 by French physicist Jean Charles Athanase Peltier, the Peltier effect describes a thermoelectric phenomenon where the junction of two different conductors or semiconductors absorbs heat on one side (cooling) and releases it on the other (heating) when an electric current flows through it.

Cooling Process

•	Heat Absorption at the Cold Side: When current flows through a Peltier module, the cold side absorbs heat, cooling the air inside the refrigerator.
•	Heat Dissipation at the Hot Side: The hot side expels the absorbed heat into the external environment through heat sinks or fans.

Temperature Control

•	Precision Regulation: Adjusting the electric current allows for precise temperature control, making Peltier modules ideal for temperature-sensitive storage applications.

Environmental Benefits

•	No Refrigerants Needed: Peltier cooling operates solely on electronic transfer, eliminating the need for traditional refrigerants like Freon, making it environmentally friendly and pollution-free.

Samsung’s Innovation in AI Hybrid Cooling

Samsung’s AI Hybrid Cooling technology masterfully combines traditional compressor systems with Peltier modules, optimized through AI algorithms to balance performance and energy efficiency.
1. Synergy Between Compressor and Peltier Modules
• The AI inverter compressor operates as the primary cooling system during normal conditions, ensuring low energy consumption.
• The Peltier module activates during peak cooling demands, such as when the door opens, delivering rapid and precise cooling to restore the internal environment.
2. AI-Driven Optimization
• Leveraging AI algorithms, the system dynamically adjusts operations based on user habits, such as door-opening frequency or restocking times, maintaining an ideal temperature consistently.
3. Improved Storage Efficiency and Capacity
• With no moving parts, the Peltier module frees up more internal space, increasing refrigerator capacity by 25 liters while also reducing operational noise.

Benefits for Consumers

Precise Temperature Control and Extended Freshness

The Peltier module’s precise temperature regulation significantly extends food freshness. Tests have shown meat stays fresh 1.4 times longer and fish 1.2 times longer compared to traditional refrigerators, catering to consumers who value quality food preservation.

Energy Savings and Cost Reduction

By delegating high-load cooling tasks to the Peltier module, the compressor experiences less strain, reducing overall energy consumption and lowering maintenance costs.

Sustainability and Environmental Impact

Samsung’s AI Hybrid Cooling technology addresses global energy efficiency and sustainability goals by reducing reliance on traditional refrigerants and optimizing energy use. This innovation supports environmentally conscious living while minimizing the product’s carbon footprint.

Looking Ahead: The Future of AI and Thermoelectric Cooling

The launch of Samsung’s AI Hybrid Cooling technology not only redefines the standards of refrigeration but also sets a template for smart upgrades in other home appliances. Combining the Peltier effect with AI technology is a significant step towards intelligent and sustainable refrigeration.

As consumer demand for energy efficiency, precise temperature control, and sustainability grows, AI Hybrid Cooling is poised to become the industry standard. Samsung has undoubtedly set a commendable benchmark through its technological innovation, leading the way in shaping smarter, more sustainable homes.

珀尔帖效应：热电现象的核心原理

珀尔帖效应是1834年由法国物理学家Jean Charles Athanase Peltier发现的一种热电效应。当直流电流通过两种不同导体或半导体的接触点时，一侧会吸热（冷却），另一侧则放热（加热）。

制冷过程

•	冷端吸热：当电流通过珀尔帖模块的半导体材料时，冷端吸收热量，将冷空气释放到冰箱内部。
•	热端散热：热端则通过散热片或风扇等装置将热量排到外部环境。

温度控制

•	精确调节：通过调整电流的大小，可以实现冷端温度的精确控制。这种能力使珀尔帖技术特别适合用于对温度敏感的食品或药品存储场景。

环保特性

•	无制冷剂：珀尔帖制冷完全基于电子转移，无需使用传统的氟利昂等制冷剂，既环保又无污染。

    中国品牌在家电市场的价格战，使LG和三星的传统零售利润空间受到挤压。租赁模式通过附加服务（如AI诊断、远程维护）提升了产品的价值，同时避免了零售商高昂的销售佣金，从而提高了企业的利润率。根据LG的财务数据，其订阅业务的利润率达10%以上，高于其传统业务的7%。
  3. 智能家居与生态系统构建：
    租赁服务的另一个显著优势是助力企业构建智能家居生态系统。LG通过ThinQ平台提供个性化功能，三星则利用SmartThings平台提供AI订阅俱乐部服务，进一步绑定用户，提升客户黏性。这不仅推动了家电联网技术的发展，也让品牌在消费者日常生活中占据更多份额。

未来趋势预测

  1. 订阅经济的增长：
    类似于Netflix和Spotify等企业推动的订阅经济模式，家电租赁正在吸引越来越多的消费者。通过灵活选择服务期限和套餐，消费者享受了更大的自由度，而企业则通过长期订阅锁定了稳定收入来源。LG和三星的全球扩展计划（如进入澳大利亚、欧洲和美国市场）进一步显示这一趋势的广阔前景。
  2. 技术创新与差异化：
    随着AI技术的普及，智能家电正从单纯硬件销售向服务集成化方向发展。例如，LG的洗衣机通过ThinQ实现功能更新，而三星的AI订阅俱乐部提供详细的月度设备诊断报告。这些创新让订阅服务不仅是“租赁”，更成为高附加值的智能服务。
  3. 竞争加剧与差异化需求：
    三星与LG在订阅服务领域的竞争已经白热化。三星推出One UI与AI订阅服务，而LG则通过优化WebOS和扩大订阅产品范围巩固市场地位。未来，市场或将出现更多定制化和增值服务，如家政服务订阅、食品配送等，从而满足消费者日益复杂的需求。

潜在挑战

尽管家电租赁模式前景可期，但仍存在潜在风险。首先，订阅模式需要企业在物流和维护方面投入更多资源，其成本可能影响利润率。其次，消费者对数据隐私的担忧可能对智能服务的推广构成阻碍。最后，如何平衡租赁服务与传统零售业务之间的关系，也将成为企业需要解决的问题。

总结

LG和三星的家电租赁服务不仅是行业内的战略调整，更是一种顺应市场需求的创新模式。随着订阅经济的深入人心、AI技术的广泛应用以及智能家居的普及，家电租赁服务极有可能成为未来家电市场的主流趋势。
