The New Gaggenau RVY497190: 5 Climate Zones and AI Cooling for a Smarter Kitchen Refrigeration

Posted on 2025-04-18

Brand History

Founded in 1683 in the Black Forest region of Germany, Gaggenau began as a blacksmith’s forge and evolved over centuries into a global symbol of luxury kitchen innovation. Since its acquisition by BSH (Bosch-Siemens Home Appliances) in 1995, Gaggenau has seamlessly combined cutting-edge technology with artistic design to produce premium kitchen appliances.

Known as “works of art for the kitchen”, Gaggenau products embody German engineering precision and the brand’s philosophy that true functionality is a form of beauty. The RVY497190 refrigerator, launched in 2025 as part of the Expressive Series, exemplifies this ideal to perfection.

Gaggenau RVY497190: 5 Climate Zones + AI Recognition & Adaptive Cooling

Overview

Debuted at EuroCucina 2024 and officially launched in Australia in 2025, the Gaggenau RVY497190 is a revolutionary fridge-freezer combination that succeeds the iconic Vario 400 series. Hailed as the next “Icon of Gaggenau,” it blends material mastery, intuitive functionality, and intelligent automation.

Key Highlights

🔄 Professional Freshness System – 5 Climate Zones

  • Five distinct climate areas: main cavity, three drawers, and freezer.
  • Independent humidity and temperature control in each zone.
  • Ethylene absorption extends freshness of fruits and vegetables.
  • Automatic modes for various food types: fruit, vegetables, meat, and more.

🤖 AI Recognition & Adaptive Cooling

  • Equipped with four internal cameras and AI algorithms.
  • Detects stored items (e.g., leafy greens, poultry, steak) and adjusts settings automatically.
  • Storage management and personalization via full touch TFT display.
  • Animated guides for maintenance and drawer interaction.

🌟 Premium Materials & Intuitive Lighting

  • Interior crafted from dark brushed stainless steel and anthracite aluminium.
  • Handleless design with door assist ensures seamless kitchen integration.
  • Warm white LED lights (3000K) intelligently adapt to ambient light and user actions.

🧊 Advanced Freezing Features

  • Freezing range from -24°C to -16°C with fast freezing down to -30°C.
  • Two fully extendable freezer drawers with cushioned closing system.
  • Integrated ice maker with fixed water connection (approx. 3 kg/day).
  • Includes removable ice storage container and scoop, with filter saturation indicators.

Core Product Specifications

  • Total volume: 576L (Fridge 379L, Freezer 197L)
  • 5 climate zones with precise humidity & temperature control
  • AI-powered object recognition and auto-programming
  • 2 Fresh climate drawers + 1 Chilled drawer
  • Frost-free cooling + activated charcoal air purification
  • Max door weight: 64 kg; drawer max: 14 kg
  • Ultra-quiet: 39 dB(A)
  • Energy efficiency: 5 Stars (AU); 354 kWh/year
  • Full-touch TFT control panel with animated guidance
  • Sabbath mode certification (Star-K)
  • Remote control & custom program storage
  • Integrated ice maker + water filtration system
  • Freezing capacity: 14kg/24h; power-outage storage time: 19h
  • LED lighting: warm white, ambient-adaptive (3000K)

Final Thoughts

More than a refrigerator, the RVY497190 is Gaggenau’s vision of the future kitchen—where technology enhances daily life, and design brings joy. With AI-driven features, customizable climate zones, luxurious materials, and smart connectivity, it transforms refrigeration into a sensory and intuitive experience.

Once again, Gaggenau proves that appliances are not just utilities, but true expressions of taste and innovation.

🧊 德国 Gaggenau：三百余年工艺与革新的代表

品牌简史

Gaggenau，创立于1683年德国黑森林地区，起初是一家铁匠铺，历经数百年的工艺演进和产品转型，最终在20世纪脱颖而出，成为全球高端厨房电器领域的代表品牌。自1995年被博世-西门子家电集团（BSH Hausgeräte）收购以来，Gaggenau 致力于将艺术设计与厨房科技深度融合，打造兼具视觉冲击与实用价值的厨房设备。

被称为“厨房中的艺术品”，Gaggenau 的产品不仅代表着德国工程技术的严谨与精密，也承载着品牌对“功能即美学”的深刻理解。而 2025 年推出的全新 Expressive 系列冰箱 RVY497190，更是这一理念的极致体现。

Gaggenau RVY497190：五独立分区 + AI 识别与自适应冷藏系统

新品概览

2024 年，Gaggenau 在米兰 EuroCucina 展会上推出了新一代冰箱产品 RVY497190，并于 2025 年在澳洲市场正式亮相。这款新品是自 2018 年 Vario 400 系列后 Gaggenau 冷藏产品线的重大升级，被誉为品牌下一个“图腾级”产品。

核心亮点

🔄 五独立分区（Professional Freshness System）

  • 冷藏室 + 三个抽屉 + 冷冻室，共五个独立气候区域。
  • 每个区域支持温湿度独立调节。
  • 配有乙烯吸收装置，有效延长果蔬保鲜期。
  • 提供针对不同食材的自动冷藏模式（如水果、蔬菜、肉类）。

🤖 AI 智能识别与自适应冷藏系统

  • 搭载四个内部摄像头与 AI 系统，可识别食材类型（绿叶菜、牛排、禽肉等）。
  • 自动匹配理想的温湿环境，提高保存效率。
  • 支持通过 TFT 屏幕进行储物管理与动画式操作引导。
  • 可保存个性化程序并远程操控。

🌟 材质工艺与灯光美学

  • 深拉丝不锈钢 + 实心深灰铝材 + 烟熏玻璃抽屉，尽显质感。
  • 无把手设计，门体自动辅助开启，融入整体厨房风格。
  • 全腔体采用暖白无眩光 LED 灯光系统，根据环境与使用状态智能调光。

🧊 高性能冷冻系统

  • 冷冻温度范围 -24°C 至 -16°C，支持极速冷冻（最低 -30°C）。
  • 冷冻仓带两层抽屉，具缓冲关闭设计。
  • 集成制冰机，日制冰量约 3 公斤。
  • 独立冰盒、可拆卸冰铲、固定水源连接，便于维护与使用。

产品核心参数摘要

  • 总容量：576L（冷藏室 379L，冷冻室 197L）
  • 专业五气候区保鲜系统
  • 智能物品识别与自动调温
  • 4 个内部摄像头 + AI 算法
  • 2 个新鲜抽屉 + 1 个冷藏抽屉（支持湿度调节）
  • 无霜技术 + 自动除霜 + 活性炭空气过滤
  • 门体承重：每扇门最大 64kg；抽屉最大 14kg
  • 噪音：39 dB（超静音）
  • 能效等级：RAP & HK 五星，年耗电量约 354kWh
  • 全触控 TFT 显示屏，支持动态引导操作
  • 支持 Sabbath Rest Mode（星K认证）
  • 远程监控与个性化配置
  • 一体式制冰机 + 滤水器 + 饱和指示
  • 冷冻能力：14kg / 24h；停电储藏时间：19h
  • 照明色温：3000K 暖白光，智能调节亮度

总结与展望

RVY497190 不仅是一台冰箱，更是 Gaggenau 对未来高端厨房生活的解答。AI 智能识别、五气候保鲜区、艺术级灯光氛围、卓越工艺——这款产品将科技与美学融合到极致。

Gaggenau 再次证明：家电不仅仅是工具，更是居家生活品味与审美的延伸。
