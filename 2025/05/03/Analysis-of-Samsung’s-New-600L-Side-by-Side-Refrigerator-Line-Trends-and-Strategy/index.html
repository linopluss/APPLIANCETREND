Analysis of Samsung’s New 600L Side-by-Side Refrigerator Line: Trends and Strategy

Posted on 2025-05-03

29/4/2025 Samsung’s latest 600L side-by-side refrigerator series marks a significant upgrade in both hardware specifications and user experience. The product range is expanding with a clear hierarchy across high, mid, and entry-level models, featuring differentiated configurations, improved energy efficiency, smarter interaction design, and an overall push toward AI integration and lifestyle enhancement.

Enhanced Product Line Strategy

Compared to the previous generation (e.g., SRS656MBFH4), the new lineup (e.g., SRS6800BFH) brings substantial improvements. The most notable advancements include:

  • Upgraded Display Technology: Samsung has introduced high-definition 21.5-inch or 9-inch TFT panels across high- and mid-end models—replacing the previous generation’s internal LED-only displays—greatly improving UI visibility and user interaction.

  • Auto Open/Close Door Technology: A standout feature now included in flagship models. Users can customize the door opening angle via the SmartThings App. There are two modes:

    • Wide-open mode: Opens the door automatically to an angle ≥80°, but requires manual closure.
    • Semi-open mode: Reduces door-opening effort and auto-closes the door after a delay when left open.

      “You can select the door opening level using the SmartThings App (SmartThings is available on Android and iOS devices. A Wi-Fi connection and a Samsung account are required). The ‘Wide-open’ option automatically opens the door by an angle of 80 degrees or more, but the door does not close automatically. The ‘Semi-open’ option reduces the force required to open the door, and automatically closes the door after a certain period of time if the door is left open. The actual angle that the doors open may vary due to the installation circumstances and usage conditions, like storing heavy items in the door bins.”

  • External Water Connection: Unlike the previous generation which used internal refillable water tanks, the new series fully adopts plumbed water dispensers, significantly reducing user maintenance effort and enhancing convenience.

  • Compressor Evolution: The new models use AI Inverter Compressors, a digitally controlled system that adapts compressor speed based on cooling demand. This ensures quieter operation, better food preservation, and improved energy performance compared to the older Digital Inverter Compressors.

  • Energy Rating Upgrades: Energy efficiency ratings are notably improved across the new series, from 3-star to 4-star in most models, aligning with the trend toward sustainable, eco-friendly appliances.

Summary & Industry Implication

From a product management perspective, Samsung’s 2025 refrigerator strategy reflects its ongoing commitment to smart home integration, AI-driven functionality, and enhanced user-centric design. The introduction of smart features such as adjustable door automation, AI compressors, and premium UI panels indicates a strong push toward positioning refrigerators not just as appliances, but as intelligent lifestyle hubs.

This evolution also aligns with macro trends in the appliance industry:

  • Stronger emphasis on smart energy management
  • Seamless SmartThings ecosystem integration
  • Enhanced user experience design (UXD)

Samsung is clearly refining its product tiers while upgrading technical foundations to address increasingly diversified household needs, setting a competitive benchmark in the premium refrigeration category.

Appendix: How the AI Inverter Compressor Works

The AI Inverter Compressor in Samsung refrigerators is a digitally controlled compressor that adapts its speed based on cooling demand. This allows for more efficient energy use and quieter operation. Key advantages include:

  • Variable Speed Control: Adjusts cooling intensity as needed, unlike traditional compressors with fixed-speed operation.
  • AI Optimization: Predicts future temperature changes based on usage patterns and environmental data, optimizing compressor workload.
  • Consistent Cooling: Reduces temperature fluctuation, improving food preservation.
  • Low Noise & Long Life: Fewer start/stop cycles reduce wear, resulting in quieter performance and extended lifespan (with up to 20-year warranty).

2025年4月29日，澳洲三星全面升级其 600L 级别对开门冰箱产品线，推出多款配置领先、交互更智能的新型号（如 SRS6800BFH），以明显区别于上一代中高端型号（如 SRS656MBFH4）。从此次产品更新节奏与策略来看，三星正加速布局以“智能、便捷、节能”为核心的新一代冰箱生态，深度响应中高端市场对于“智慧家电”与“生活美学”的双重需求。

一、屏幕升级：9 寸与 21.5 寸 TFT 屏全线覆盖

相比上一代产品仅限旗舰款搭载 21.5 寸 TFT 屏，新系列显著提升了屏幕配置覆盖率。即便是中配型号，也配备了 9 寸或 21.5 寸高清 TFT 屏，不仅支持菜谱查看、内容同步和内部摄像头实时显示，还通过 SmartThings 实现更多交互，强化了用户的使用参与感和厨房智能体验。

二、智能开门/关门设计，强化人机交互体验

新款高配型号（如 SRS6800BFH）引入了“自动开门/关门”功能，显著提升了交互便捷性。用户可通过 SmartThings App 自定义选择开门角度：

  • Wide-open 模式：自动将门打开至 80 度以上，适合搬放大件食材；
  • Semi-open 模式：轻触开门，冰箱会在用户离开后自动关闭，防止忘关门造成冷气流失。

注：实际开门角度会因安装环境和门内储物重量等因素有所不同。

该功能不仅提升了冰箱的人性化设计，也在实际使用场景中有效减少了能耗与温度波动，体现了家电与 AI 算法结合的“主动智能”。

三、制冷核心：AI Inverter Compressor 带来节能与保鲜并重

新款机型普遍采用 AI Inverter Compressor，这是三星引入 AI 算法优化的数字变频压缩机技术。其核心原理如下：

AI Inverter Compressor 原理简述：

  • 利用 AI 分析用户使用习惯，预测温度变化；
  • 实时调整压缩机转速，提升控温精度；
  • 降低噪音与磨损，提高能效与产品寿命。

相较传统压缩机，该技术能效提升高达 40%，为用户带来更稳定的冷藏体验，并帮助新系列产品能效等级普遍提升至 4 星。

四、供水系统升级：从“内接”到“外接”更利用户长期维护

上一代产品普遍采用内接水系统（冰箱内置水盒），存在清洁频繁、水质变化快等问题。新系列全部采用外接水系统（Plumbed），用户连接家用水管后可持续供水制冰，无需手动加水，更卫生也更便捷。
