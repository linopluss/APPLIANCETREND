32” Display + Auto Open Door – Samsung 809L BESPOKE AI Cross-Door Fridge Launches in Australia

Posted on 2025-04-18

In April 2025, Samsung officially launched its flagship 809L BESPOKE AI Family Hub™+ Cross-Door Refrigerator (model SRF9900BFH) in the Australian market. This innovative product had already drawn worldwide attention at its debut during CES 2024, where it showcased groundbreaking features in smart kitchen technology.

Now, after a year of anticipation, it is finally available to consumers—signaling the arrival of a new age in intelligent refrigeration.

From CES Concept to Market Reality

At CES 2024, Samsung impressed the world with this refrigerator’s advanced features:

  • AI Vision Inside: Internal cameras capable of identifying up to 33 fresh food items and helping manage your fridge inventory.
  • 32” Bezel-less TFT Touch Display: Powered by the upgraded AI Family Hub™+ system for rich interactions and digital experiences.
  • Auto Open Door: Doors that open with a light touch—ideal for busy or multitasking users.

Now available in Australia, this smart fridge delivers every promise made at CES—demonstrating Samsung’s innovation capability.

Cross-Door Design with Huge 809L Capacity

With 809 liters of storage, this fridge offers one of the largest capacities in the market. The cross-door configuration efficiently separates refrigeration and freezing areas: refrigerators on top, freezers at the bottom, left-right symmetry for smart zoning.

The built-in water dispenser and spherical ice maker, combined with door-side storage, add even more flexibility for modern households.

AI Vision Inside: From Identification to Smart Management

AI Vision Inside is more than just a camera. It recognizes items, updates the inventory automatically, and allows you to set “use-by” dates manually. It even suggests recipes based on what’s inside.

Think of it as a personal food assistant built right into your fridge.

AI Family Hub™+: A New Digital Command Center

The upgraded 32” bezel-less display offers 2.1x more screen area than the previous Family Hub™ model. It acts as a central smart display for your kitchen, showing:

  • Daily schedules
  • Weather and news
  • Media content
  • Voice assistant control

This refrigerator is not just an appliance—it’s a home coordination hub.

Auto Open Door: Convenience in a Tap

With just a light touch, the refrigerator doors open automatically. This feature is particularly useful when your hands are full or you’re multitasking.

This function applies only to the upper refrigerator doors. It is turned off by default and can be enabled via the control panel. Note: door opening angles may vary depending on conditions such as installation or heavy door bins. There is no auto-close function.

Conclusion: The Future Is Now for Smart Refrigeration

The Samsung 809L BESPOKE AI Family Hub™+ isn’t just a refrigerator—it’s a lifestyle partner. Combining futuristic design, AI-powered intelligence, and practical functionality, it redefines how we interact with our kitchens.

The kitchen of the future is no longer a passive space for storage or cooking—it’s a smart, connected living hub.

With technologies like AI Vision and expanded smart interfaces, refrigerators will play an increasingly central role in how families live, plan, and thrive.

2025年4月，三星在澳大利亚市场正式推出了其旗舰新品——809L BESPOKE AI Family Hub™+ 十字门冰箱（型号 SRF9900BFH）。这款产品早在 2024年CES国际消费电子展 上首次亮相时，就吸引了全球媒体与家电爱好者的高度关注。如今，它终于来到消费者身边，开启了冰箱智慧化发展的新纪元。

从概念到落地：一年等待，值得拥有

在2024年CES上，三星展示了这款冰箱的核心技术，包括：

  • AI Vision Inside：通过内置摄像头识别多达33种新鲜食材，辅助管理食物储存；
  • 32英寸TFT无边框触控大屏：全新AI Family Hub™+系统，实现更丰富的交互与娱乐功能；
  • Auto Open Door轻触即开：即便双手被占用，也能轻松开门，提升生活便利性。

一年之后，这款产品终于在澳洲上市，并保留了原型机中的所有亮点功能，验证了三星在智能家电领域的技术成熟度。

超大容量，满足家庭所需

冰箱总容量高达809L，是当前市场中极具竞争力的产品之一。十字门结构将冷藏与冷冻区科学分布，上层为冷藏室，下层为冷冻室，左右对称设计，既节省空间，也便于分类储存。

内置饮水机与球形制冰机、门侧收纳系统进一步提升了整机的使用体验，满足现代家庭多样化的生活需求。

AI Vision Inside：不只是识别，更是提醒与建议

AI Vision Inside 功能并不只是“看见”食物，它还能“理解”用户行为。当你放入或取出食物时，系统会自动识别物品，并更新内部食物清单。你可以手动设置保质期，系统会提醒你及时使用，并给出菜谱建议，避免浪费。

它不仅是一个智能识别系统，更是你的家庭食材助理。

AI Family Hub™+：厨房的智能中枢

升级后的 AI Family Hub™+ 配备了32英寸无边框大屏，相比上一代 Family Hub™ 提升了 2.1 倍的可视面积。它不仅可以显示天气、家庭日程、新闻和音乐播放，还能提供个性化推荐、智能语音控制和远程管理功能。

这是厨房中的数字信息中心，也是家庭连接的纽带。

Auto Open Door：轻轻一碰，科技响应

轻轻一碰门体，冰箱自动开启。这个功能尤其适用于手中拿满东西或操作不便的情况，大大提升了用户日常使用的便捷性和人性化体验。

此功能仅适用于上方冷藏门，默认关闭，用户可在面板中开启。注意门体开启角度可能因安装环境和门内重物而异，且无自动关闭功能。

总结：AI冰箱不再是“未来”，而是“现在”

三星809L BESPOKE AI Family Hub™+ 是一款集智能识别、人性化交互与空间美学于一体的高端冰箱产品。它不仅重塑了厨房电器的定义，也展现了AI如何服务于真实生活场景。

未来的厨房，不再只是储物与烹饪的空间，而是智能家庭生活的核心节点。

随着AI Vision、IoT生态与人机交互不断完善，冰箱将成为家庭健康、信息管理与生活灵感的综合入口。
