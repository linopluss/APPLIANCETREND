LG Unveils Second-Generation SIGNATURE Lineup at CES 2025

Posted on 2025-01-07 Lino Edited on 2025-04-18

On December 29, 2024, LG Electronics announced the launch of its second-generation LG SIGNATURE lineup at CES 2025. Presented under the “Live Beyond” theme, the new lineup highlights the brand’s ultra-premium positioning and technological innovations. This series integrates cutting-edge technology and sophisticated design to enhance user experience and create a more connected smart home environment.

The new LG SIGNATURE lineup continues its minimalist aesthetics and outstanding performance, incorporating AI-driven features and intuitive operation. The series includes the LG SIGNATURE French Door Refrigerator, Smart InstaView Microwave, Double Oven Induction Range, Dishwasher, Washing Machine, and Dryer. The French Door Refrigerator features a transparent T-OLED door panel that combines a touch interface and InstaView panel, integrated with the ThinQ™ Food Management System and an AI camera for food recognition and recipe suggestions. The Smart Microwave and Induction Range enhance cooking experiences through built-in cameras and Gourmet AI technology, offering even entertainment content.

The LG SIGNATURE Dishwasher and Washing Machine series represent significant advancements in design and functionality. The dishwasher features a concealed handle and QuadWash™ Pro technology, while the washing machine series utilizes AI DD™ 2.0 technology and inverter heat pump technology for precise fabric care and efficient low-temperature drying.

At CES 2025, LG will collaborate with Italian furniture brand Molteni&C to showcase these products in premium lifestyle spaces. The LG ThinQ ON AI Home Hub will demonstrate the convenience of smart home living to visitors.

2024年12月29日，LG电子宣布将在2025年国际消费电子展（CES 2025）上推出其第二代LG SIGNATURE产品线。此次发布会以“Live Beyond”为主题，展示了该公司超高端品牌的定位和技术创新。这一系列产品整合了前沿科技和精美设计，旨在提升用户体验并创造一个连接更紧密的智能家居环境。

新一代LG SIGNATURE产品线延续了其简约美学和卓越性能，加入了AI驱动功能和直观的操作。此系列包括LG SIGNATURE法式门冰箱、智能Instaview微波炉、双烤箱电感应炉、洗碗机、洗衣机及烘干机等产品。其中，法式门冰箱具备透明的T-OLED门板，集触控界面和InstaView面板于一身，同时嵌入了ThinQ™食材管理系统和AI摄像头，为用户提供食材识别、食谱建议等功能。智能微波炉和电感应炉则通过内置摄像头和Gourmet AI技术提升烹饪体验，甚至可以提供娱乐内容。

LG SIGNATURE洗碗机和洗衣机系列则在设计和功能上实现了重大突破。洗碗机采用了隐藏式把手和QuadWash™ Pro技术，洗衣机系列搭载AI DD™ 2.0技术和变频热泵技术，实现了精准的织物护理和高效的低温烘干。

在CES 2025上，LG将与意大利家具品牌Molteni&C合作，展示这些产品在高端生活空间中的套系化应用。同时，LG ThinQ ON AI家居中心将向参观者展示智能家居生活的便捷性。
